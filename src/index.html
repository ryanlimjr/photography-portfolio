About Me

Hello there! I see you have landed on my webpage. I am Ryan, a hobbyist photographer based in singapore. I enjoy taking portraits of people as well as doing sports photography from time to time.

Starting out when I was 12, I used my father's point and shoot digital camera then to capture memories of my first kayaking competition with my school team. Ever since, photography has been an avenue to express my creativity.

Each photograph I take is a carefully orchestrated creative process, from the location in the shoot to the relationships formed with the people I work with. I firmly believe that connecting with your surroundings and the people you meet is what makes a photograph beautiful.

Join me as I take you on a journey through my lens!
